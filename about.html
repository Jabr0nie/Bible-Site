About 0xBible.faith

0xbible.faith was created to preserve the Bible on the blockchain forever, ensuring God’s word stays open, uncensored, and true for the world to access.

Every verse is stored in a smart contract on Conflux eSpace, a fast and secure blockchain. Choose any book, chapter, or verse to see it pulled live from the chain—no servers, just scripture’s truth. You can verify the contract on ConfluxScan.

Why blockchain? It can’t be shut down or changed, unlike traditional databases, making it a digital sanctuary for the Bible. Whether you’re drawn by faith, curiosity, or technology, this project exists to keep scripture alive in the digital era for everyone, everywhere.

Explore a verse and share its link to spread God’s word from the blockchain to the world.
